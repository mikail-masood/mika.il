toiletnearU

In Progress (stable build to launch Jan 2021)

A personal project dear to my heart. This project was made possible due to the collaborative efforts of my sister, Marium Masood, and good friend, Jamie Zhou, who both helped immeasurably from the inception of the idea to the finished product.

The app is designed to not only help students find washrooms on campus, but also to; rate the facilities, report any issues (missing toilet paper, overflowing urinal, etc), and review the washrooms so that other users can be better informed when making this tough decision.

In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, it is essential that high use facilities such as on campus washrooms are consitently maintained to a very high standard. The facility rating system gives students the information they need to keep themselves safe from unhygenic or overcrowded areas once the campus reopens.

The flagging system provides a way to notify other students of immediate concerns, such as overflowing urinals, or empty toilet paper stalls. This will also notify the faculty to address issues as swfitly as possible.

Stay tuned for more information on features and launch dates!
